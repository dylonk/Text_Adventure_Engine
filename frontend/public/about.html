About Text Adventure Creation Tool

Welcome to the Text Adventure Creation Tool, a web-based platform designed to make creating interactive text adventure games accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

Our goal is to empower aspiring game creators, educators, writers, and gamers with a tool that removes the technical barriers of game development. With our tool, you can bring your stories to life through interactive, branching narratives—no programming skills required.

Key Features

  • Intuitive Interface: Easily create and manage rooms, dialogues, and decision paths to build a dynamic game world.
  • Media Integration: Upload images and audio to enhance your game’s sensory experience.
  • Accessibility Options: Configure text-to-speech, adjustable text sizes, and high-contrast modes to ensure inclusivity for all players.
  • Collaboration: Work together with others to create games, share ideas, and edit projects in real-time.
  • Export Functionality: Export your games as standalone web applications, allowing others to play your creation independently.

Who It's For

This tool is perfect for anyone interested in creating interactive storytelling experiences, including:

  • Aspiring game creators
  • Educators creating learning tools
  • Writers exploring interactive fiction
  • Gamers who want to try their hand at game creation
  • People with disabilities, as we’ve built accessibility into the foundation of our tool

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Accessibility is at the core of our design. Our tool complies with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, supporting users with visual, auditory, and motor impairments to ensure that game creation is open to everyone.

Get Started!

Dive into the world of text adventures today and start creating your own interactive stories. Whether you’re a seasoned storyteller or just starting out, the Text Adventure Creation Tool makes it easy to bring your ideas to life.
